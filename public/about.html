The Origin

How I Got Here

I'm an AI-first founder from India and part of the founding team at SEOengine.ai. Over the last few years, I watched brilliant domain experts — coaches and consultants — hit a brutal ceiling.

They weren't failing because they lacked clients. They were failing because their business backend was held together with manual effort and willpower.

Every new client meant 3 hours of onboarding. Every lost lead was due to a missed manual follow-up. They had built incredible expertise, but terrible systems. I realised AI could fix this — reliably, invisibly, and instantly.

The Philosophy

What I Believe

Your business should run even when you're not in the room. If it doesn't, you don't own a business — you own a highly demanding job.

Automation isn't about being lazy. It's about buying your time back to do the 10% of tasks only you can do. The rest belongs to the machine.
