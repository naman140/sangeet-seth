Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so, for example, to provide our Services, to communicate with you, or to make our Services better. We collect information you provide directly to us (such as name and email when you book an audit).

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to deliver our consulting and automation services, communicate with you about your automation audits and projects, and send you our weekly dispatch (if you opted in).

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell our users' private personal information. We share information only in limited circumstances (such as with third-party vendors who need to know information to provide their services to us) and with your clear consent when setting up third-party automation software on your behalf.

4. Security

While no online service is 100% secure, we work very hard to protect information about you against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction.

5. Contact Us

If you have a question about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Sangeet's official email address provided during our initial consultations.
